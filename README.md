# ArtRaja — Free & Premium Artwork Platform

ArtRaja is a structured digital artwork platform providing free and premium creative assets including AI-generated art, wallpapers, icons, backgrounds, and design resources. The platform is built to help creators, developers, designers, and content makers access high-quality artwork in one place.

## Platform Sections

### Free Artwork
Free artwork section includes downloadable creative assets available for personal and creative use. This section is continuously updated with new designs and visual resources.

Includes:
- AI Generated Art
- Wallpapers
- Backgrounds
- Icons
- Design Elements
- Creative Assets
- Digital Illustrations

Users can directly preview and download artwork without login.

### Premium Artwork
Premium artwork section includes curated design packs and high-quality assets. These resources are organized for creators who need advanced visual content.

Includes:
- Premium AI Art Packs
- Poster Packs
- UI Graphics
- Social Media Kits
- NFT Style Artwork
- Illustration Packs
- Design Bundles

Premium artwork may require external unlock or support access.

## Platform Goals
